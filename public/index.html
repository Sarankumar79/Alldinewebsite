Welcome to Alldine.

Have you ever been stuck in a meeting that refuses to end and started eating into your break time? Do you wish that you could go to the food court and have hot food ready to be served to you? Not to worry, you are at the right place.

About

Alldine is created by an MBA student at IIM Raipur. Alldine is an online food ordering app which works primarily to reduce the time between kitchen to table. Our main motto is to deliver high experience of food ordering without losing the authenticity and quality of the food.
